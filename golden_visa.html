Categories and Eligibility

Investors

Real Estate Investors: Can obtain a visa for 5 or 10 years by investing in property in the UAE. The minimum investment amount varies; for a 10-year visa, it typically involves a higher investment in real estate or a public investment fund. Public Investments: Including investments in shares, investment funds, and other options specified by the UAE government.

Entrepreneurs

Those who own or partner in a startup registered in the country in the category of small and medium enterprises (SMEs), and who have a successful project with a minimum capital of AED 500,000, can apply for a 5-year visa.

Specialized Talents and Professionals

Highly skilled workers and professionals in science, engineering, health, education, business management, and technology with a valid employment contract in the UAE can apply, often requiring a minimum educational level and salary criteria.

Scientists and Researchers

Recognized for their contribution and research in their field. Eligibility often requires recommendations from recognized UAE entities.

Outstanding Students and Graduates

Students with excellent academic performance in the UAE and abroad can qualify for a 5-year visa. This includes university and high school students.

Humanitarian Pioneers

Including award-winning individuals in various fields, influential figures in culture and art, and other categories that the UAE considers for special contributions.

Application Process

Determine Eligibility: Review the specific criteria for your category to ensure eligibility.

Gather Required Documents: Including investments in shares, investment funds, and other options specified by the UAE government.

Determine Eligibility: Apply through the Federal Authority for Identity and Citizenship (ICA), the General Directorate of Residency and Foreigners Affairs (GDRFA) in Dubai, or through officially recognized real estate developers in the UAE for real estate investments.

Application Submission: Including investments in shares, investment funds, and other options specified by the UAE government.

Pay Fees: Application fees vary based on the visa duration and category. Visa Issuance: Upon approval, the Golden Visa will be issued, allowing you to enjoy the benefits of long-term residency in the UAE.
